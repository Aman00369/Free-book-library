Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our digital library

Are these books really free to download?
Yes! All books in our collection are completely free to download. We focus on public domain works and titles that have been made freely available by authors or publishers.
What formats are the books available in?
Most books are available in PDF format, but many titles also come in EPUB and MOBI formats to accommodate different e-readers and devices.
Can I suggest books to be added to the library?
Absolutely! We welcome suggestions from our community. Please use the contact form to recommend titles, and we'll do our best to add them to our collection if they're available in the public domain.
Do I need to create an account to download books?
No, you don't need to create an account to download books. However, creating a free account allows you to bookmark favorites, track your reading history, and receive personalized recommendations.
